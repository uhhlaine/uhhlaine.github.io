What is Climate Change and why do we have to stop it?



Climate Change, also known as global warming, is an alteration in global or regional weather patterns caused mainly because of human-induced emissions of greenhouse gases. From the burning of about 35 billion tonnes of carbon dioxide a year, to deforestation and unsustainable agriculture, climate change accelerates. The consequences are devastating.

As temperatures continue to rise, plant and animal species decline, contributing to the greater issue of biodiversity loss.
